  • Exercise 5: Determine the luminous intensity of the unknown light source.
    • The luminous intensity is calculated using the inverse square law:
      Ix / 0.65² = Is / 0.35²
      Substituting Is = 40 cd, we find:
      Ix ≈ 138 cd
    Luminous intensity of unknown source: 138 cd.
