  • Exercise 3: Calculate the irradiance received by the eye.
    • The solid angle subtended by the pupil at a distance of 1 m from the light source is calculated as:
      Ω ≈ 0.006 / 2π sr
    • The irradiance (E) is the product of the solid angle and radiant intensity:
      E ≈ 5.82 × 10-11 W
    Irradiance received by the eye: 5.82 × 10-11 W.
