  • Exercise 4: Calculate the irradiance and illuminance at the table from a 200 W light bulb emitting red light (650 nm) placed 2 m above the table.
    • The irradiance at the table is:
      I = (ε × P) / 4πr²
      Substituting the values gives:
      E ≈ 2.5 / π W/m²
    • The illuminance is calculated using:
      Illuminance = Irradiance × 685 × V(λ)
      where V(λ) is the luminous efficiency at 650 nm, which is 0.1. Therefore:
      Illuminance ≈ 21.8 lux
    Irradiance: 2.5 / π W/m².
    Illuminance: 21.8 lux.
