  • Question 6: The radiance of a diffuse light source is 5000 W/m²·sr. Calculate the radiant exitance and energy emitted.
    • Radiant exitance for a diffuse source is given by:
      M = L × π = 5000 × π ≈ 15700 W/m²
    • The energy emitted is:
      Energy = L × A × π ≈ 157 W
    Radiant exitance: 15700 W/m².
    Energy emitted: 157 W.
